Application Overview

This application will follow the traditional style of hangman. This is a guessing game played with two or more players, with a limited number of guesses. One player would pick a word, and the other player(s) try to guess the word letter by letter. Each letter a player gets wrong, a body part is added to the "hangman" until the player runs out of guesses when the "hangman" is completed.
